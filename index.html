Empowering Education Foundation

Advocating for the Education of the Future

A cultural immersion program connecting developing countries of the world together under a common cause.

The Empowering Education Foundation is a non-profit that hopes to bridge the education gap through tutoring led initiatives. It is a national organization founded by the endeavors of high school students striving for a better world with opportunity for all.

Fostering the Learning of Today for the Action of Tomorrow

Our mission is to create an impact on student’s lives throughout the globe towards a desire for the pursuit of knowledge, which will be quantified through higher academic performance, cultural competency, and pursuit of higher education.

As population rises on the global scale, the number of young children without access to quality education increases. Our goal is to expose children in developing countries with access to multiple disciplines that could enhance their future careers, as we strive to learn more about each other's cultures and grow together. This program is not meant to teach a student rigidly, but develop them with new experiences that will benefit students on both sides of the initiative.
